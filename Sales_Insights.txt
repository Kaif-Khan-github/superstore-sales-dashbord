
----Sales Dashboard Insights – Superstore Dataset------

1. ##Technology has the highest profit margin:
   - Even with fewer total sales than Furniture, it brings the most profit.
   - Great performance in high-margin items like accessories and phones.

2. ## Furniture drives high sales but low profit:
   - Particularly sub-categories like Bookcases and Tables show negative or very low profit.
   - Indicates a need to optimize cost or pricing.

3. ## Office Supplies are stable but low-impact:
   - Moderate sales and very small profits.
   - Could consider bundling or discount strategies to boost impact.

4. ## Region-wise gap:
   - The West region leads in both Sales and Profit.
   - The South underperforms in profit — maybe needs local marketing or cost control.
